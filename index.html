About

Teacher & Mentor

I'm Kieran and I have been a qualified teacher since 2009 teaching in both public and private schools with students aged 11-18. I started my career in England where I taught middle school and high school math including Advanced level further mathematics and test prep for Oxford and Cambridge universities. I moved to the US in 2017 and have taught mainly PreCalculus Honours and AP Calculus AB/BC as well as some Algebra I and II.

I first got into teaching at the age of 15 when I qualified as a Trampoline Gymnastics coach and later became a national coach and judge. I have always enjoyed helping others improve their skills and achieve their potential. In college, I majored in mathematics and moved on to a graduate degree in mathematics education.

Currently, I am available for math tutoring in a range of subjects from Algebra I up to AP Calculus BC and everything in between. I also have experience with IB (international baccalaureate) standard and higher level, UK courses for KS3, GCSE, and A'Level as well as college admissions exams for Oxford and Cambridge.

I understand that every student has different needs and expectations for tutoring sessions. You may need specific help with a homework assignment, preparation for tests/quizzes, reviewing work missed due to absence from school, or maybe you want to get ahead and learn something new. We will work together to ensure you get what you need.

Services

Home tutoring/schooling (Calgary)

We are located in NW Calgary and offer home services throughout Calgary. We can also provide full year courses for home schooling.

Online tutoring (Worldwide)

Online sessions are available worldwide via Zoom. Each session is recorded and can be reviewed at any time via google classroom.

Personalised google classroom

Every student gets their own personalised google classroom page which hosts copies of all the notes we make as well as recordings of sessions for online students. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor work on google classroom.

Test prep

Get intensive practice for class test and standardised tests such as SAT, ACT, AP, IB, and many more.

Network Science

Exclusive full year or half year network science course only available here. We work in collaboration with University of Calgary Professor Dr. Emma Towlson to provide a unique research experience in network science.

Referral Programme

All clients will recieve a referral code. You get one free session for each new paying customer who uses your referral code.
